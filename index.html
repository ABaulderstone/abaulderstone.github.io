About me

I’m a Junior Developer based in Sydney, currently enrolled in a Diploma of IT delivered in a Bootcamp format. I’m learning Ruby on Rails, HTML/CSS, JavaScript and the MERN Stack

Seeking a change in career, I moved from Adelaide, to Sydney in early 2019. This has also allowed me an opportunity to focus on personal development and start fresh.

As a Developer, I want to merge two of my biggest passions; technology and social justice. I want to use the skills I’ve honed in order to amplify marginalised voices, improve accessibility, and generally make the world a more inclusive place.
